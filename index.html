About us

Founded in 2015, KTH Learning merged with StudioFive10 in 2020 to create a holistic hub for learning and creativity.

Our philosophy

We believe in "learning by doing." Our approach combines rigorous academics with project-based creative work. Every student builds confidence, resilience, and critical thinking.
